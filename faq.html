Know Before You Go

Frequently Asked Questions

Power & Currency

  • Outlets: 120 volts (Standard U.S. plugs).
  • Currency: U.S. dollar is local; Euros and Yen are widely accepted.
  • Local banks provide full currency exchange services.

Safety & Health

  • Violent crime is very rare; standard precautions against petty theft are advised.
  • One full-service hospital (multilingual staff) and several clinics are available.

Alcohol & Language

  • Alcohol: Sales/service prohibited between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • Age: Minimum drinking age is 18.
  • Language: English is fluent among youth/town residents; limited in rural areas.
How do national holidays affect my trip?

Taniti celebrates many national holidays throughout the year. During these times, some attractions and restaurants may close. We recommend checking the local calendar or asking your lodging provider when finalizing your 2026 travel dates.
